The beginning

My coding journey started in 2015 when I wanted to create a website (dharmazdarma.cz) for my Buddhist publishing project Dharma Zdarma.

In 2020 I started another Buddhist project. And again, I did need a website. I tweaked a Wordpress template and created the first version of my Pandita.cz website (it is not live anymore). That was the first time I thought that I could make a living of my coding skills.

STATIC SITE GENERATORS

For my Buddhist activities I also needed a blog. But I did not want to use Wordpress anymore. I decided to use Github Pages and created the How to Create Pandita blog. To create this blog, I used Jekyll.

I was quite happy with Github Pages and I decided create a new version of my Pandita.cz website. But I did not like Jekyll so much because the instalation (all the Ruby gems) was complicated and for a bigger website the build time could be just too long. Eventually I decided to use HUGO instead of Jekyll.

ABOUT ME

20 years ago, I graduated from the Charles University in Prague. Economics was my major. During my studies, I was interested in data analysis. I even wrote my thesis on regression analysis.

For many years I was very sick with chronic fatigue syndrome and I was not able to work. Two years ago my health gradually started to improve. I am still quite far from full recovery but I feel good enough to look for some part time job.

And I almost forgot. I am from the Czech Republic and I live in the Czech Republic. For many years, I lived outside of the country and I am fluent in English. You can hear me speaking English in this Clever Navbar video.
